Mods updated after this date might have an outdated review below.
ATTENTION:NEW GAME VERSION! The game has been updated. Various mods will not work with this new game version. Take care to get the most recent updates of all mods, and carefully read your report. It is possible you may even need to switch and subscribe to a new alternative version of a mod. If your city doesn't work right, do NOT overwrite its save! It might be wise to use "-noWorkshop" as start parameter to play pure Vanilla in a new city until all mods are updated or alternatives are available. (Warning: Saves that used mods may not load in vanilla mode!) See https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=466981085 for further information. A list of broken mods is also available at https://pdxint.at/BrokenModCS

General information

  • Always EXIT TO DESKTOP and restart the game. Never exit to main menu!
  • NEVER (un)subscribe to anything while the game is running! This resets some mods.
  • When playing with mods, save to a new savegame often and make frequent BACKUPS.
  • Always READ the mod description on the Steam Workshop before subscribing.
  • Having issues with a mod? Make a comment on its Workshop page so the author knows.
  • Abandoned mods can still work fine. They're just unlikely to get updates.
  • Mod compatible, but not working? Try unsubscribe and resubscribe (while not in game).
  • Found a mistake? Please fill out this form.
